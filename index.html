How it works

Three messages to a better handle on your money.

No app to download, no forms to fill. Bachat Sathi lives inside a chat you already have open.

01

Say hello, in your language

Message the number and start typing in Roman Urdu, English, Sindhi or Sinhala — Bachat Sathi replies in the same language automatically.

02

Talk through your income and expenses

Tell it what you earn and spend, in your own words. It asks the right follow-up questions, the way a good sathi (companion) would.

03

Get a plan you can actually follow

Bachat Sathi turns the conversation into practical, honest savings guidance — sized to your real income, not a textbook budget.

Why it exists

Most financial advice was never built for WhatsApp-first households.

Bachat Sathi was built to close that gap — for daily-wage earners, small shopkeepers, students and families managing money without a financial advisor.

No jargon, no forms

Just a conversation, the way you'd explain your budget to a trusted friend.

Built for the region

Language, tone and examples are grounded in Pakistan, India and Sri Lanka — not translated from elsewhere.

Free to use

Bachat Sathi doesn't charge users. It's currently in early testing ahead of a wider rollout.

Private by default

Your conversation is used only to help you. Read the Privacy Policy for details.

Good to know

Frequently asked questions

Is Bachat Sathi free to use?
Yes. Bachat Sathi does not charge users to chat with it. It's currently being tested with a small group of users ahead of a wider public launch.
Is this real financial advice?
Bachat Sathi offers general budgeting and savings guidance for educational purposes. It is not a licensed financial advisor, and its guidance should not replace advice from a qualified professional for major financial decisions.
What happens to the information I share?
Your messages are used to give you relevant coaching and are not sold to third parties. See the Privacy Policy for full details.
Which languages does it support?
Roman Urdu, English, Sindhi and Sinhala today, with more languages planned as Bachat Sathi grows.
Is it available outside WhatsApp?
Not yet. Bachat Sathi currently works on WhatsApp only.

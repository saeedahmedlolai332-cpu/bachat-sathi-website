Privacy Policy

How Bachat Sathi collects, uses and protects your information.

Last updated: July 2026

Bachat Sathi is currently in early testing with a limited group of users. This policy will be reviewed and updated as the service grows.

1. Who we are

Bachat Sathi is an AI financial coaching service delivered over WhatsApp, operated by Saeed Ahmed Lolai, based in Ghotki, Sindh, Pakistan. This policy explains what information Bachat Sathi collects when you message it, and how that information is used.

2. Information we collect

When you message Bachat Sathi on WhatsApp, we may collect and store:

  • Your WhatsApp phone number
  • The messages you send to the bot, and the bot's replies
  • Your language preference (for example, Roman Urdu, English, Sindhi or Sinhala)
  • Any feedback or suggestions you choose to share

We do not ask for or knowingly collect sensitive information such as your CNIC number, bank account details, card numbers, or passwords. Please avoid sharing this kind of information in chat.

3. How we use your information

Information you share is used only to:

  • Hold a conversation with you and respond to your messages
  • Remember your language preference across the conversation
  • Improve the quality of Bachat Sathi's coaching over time
  • Review feedback to fix problems and prioritize improvements

We do not sell your information to third parties, and we do not use it for advertising.

4. How your messages are processed

Bachat Sathi uses WhatsApp's official Business Platform (provided by Meta) to send and receive messages, and Anthropic's Claude API to generate responses. Your messages are sent to these services in order to power the conversation. Each of these providers has its own privacy practices governing how they handle data passed through their platforms.

5. Data storage and retention

Conversation data and feedback are stored securely on our hosting provider for as long as needed to operate and improve the service. We aim to keep only what is necessary and may periodically remove data that is no longer needed.

6. Your choices

You can stop using Bachat Sathi at any time by simply not messaging the number again. If you would like your data deleted, you can request this using the contact details below.

7. Children's privacy

Bachat Sathi is intended for adults managing their own finances. It is not directed at children, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Changes to this policy

As Bachat Sathi grows, this policy may be updated to reflect new features or requirements. Material changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
